Instead of being pulled down by the situation, I decided to take an empowering path forward: transitioning into a Content Strategist and Copywriter.

Every day is a challenge and I'm constantly juggling tasks, interests and ideas - but it's all worth it when I can see how my work impacts clients and makes a meaningful difference in the world of marketing.

  • What is your greatest strength?

    As someone with a passion for English language and literature, the ability to express my ideas through the written word is my greatest strength. It's something that I take pride in and strive to sharpen every single day.

    But I don't stop there - I believe in going further by bringing those ideas to life through visuals too! That's why I'm familiar with programs like Adobe Creative Suite and Figma, which allow me to craft vibrant expressions of my ideas in both written and visual formats. Whether it's a creative writing project or an artful endeavor, these experiences make me the perfect candidate for any task requiring innovation and creativity!

  • What is your weakness?

    I tend to overthink. I would work on a project and then suddenly start mulling over each detail of the work, leading me to worry about making mistakes and second-guessing my decisions.

    My tendency to overthink and overdo things can sometimes be a blessing, as I strive for excellence, but it can also become a hindrance, causing me to spend too much effort on the smallest details and struggle to stay focused.

    To overcome this issue, I am learning how to break down challenges into achievable steps, be clearer with my intentions, and focus on producing quality work instead of getting stuck in an endless cycle of overthinking.
